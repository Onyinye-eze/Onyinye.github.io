Projects

Nigeria's GDP Analysis 2022

This analysis was done to understand the quarterly trend and forecast of Nigeria's GDP from 2020 to 2022. It was also important to analyze the activity sector groups and their various contributions to both real and nominal GDP. Data was first structured in Excel and then transformed in PowerBi Power Query after which analysis and visualisation was finalised to give insights.

Global Retail Store Analysis

Using dataset that was collected over a period of three years (2019 - 2021), this data was cleaned and transformed using PowerBi Power Query. With the clean dataset, I built a dashboard which visualizes insights to answer questions pertaining to profit and business expansion.

Analysis of American Airlines (Flight Delays and Cancellations)

A total of approximately six million flights records from 2015 were cleaned and analysed to show most popular airline, most travelled day of the week, number of cancelled flights and more.

Covid-19 Impact on Taffic in Australia in 2020

This analysis was done to get insights on issues such as death rate, trend of Covid Cases over a period of time and ultimately its effect on traffic.

Analysis on Global Shark Attacks

For this project, I used a dataset that details reported shark attacks globally over a period of 120 years. Data was cleaned in Excel Power Query and visualization was done to the trend of shark attacks over 120 years, the most injured body parts, the time of the day during which one is likely to get attacked, the countries that reported the most attacks.etc.

World Energy Consumption

The Dataset used is a collection of key metrics maintained by https://ourworldindata.org/energy which is from various Energy sources such as SHIFT Data Portal, BP Statistical Review of World Energy, EMBER - Global Electricity and more. Analysis was done to get insights into consumption of energy by different countries
